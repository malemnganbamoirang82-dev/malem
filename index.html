Flagship Product

Meet Recova AI

The AI-powered debt recovery platform built to help every lending organization recover more, chase less, and spend less — without the manual grind.

Powered by
Malem

AI-Powered Collections,
Redefined.

Recova AI modernizes debt recovery through intelligent automation, smarter workflows, and multi-channel communication — transforming how organizations collect what they are owed.

📞
Automated Calls & WhatsApp
Consistent, timely follow-ups with every borrower through automated calls and WhatsApp messages sent from your own number — so borrowers trust and respond.
💳
Instant Payment Links
Send payment links directly in follow-up messages, making it frictionless for borrowers to pay immediately — increasing repayment rates dramatically.
⚡
Chase Less Automation
Automates the entire follow-up process — from reminders to payment tracking — so your team spends minimal time chasing borrowers manually.
💰
Spend Less, Recover More
By reducing manual calling, repeated follow-ups, and field visits, Recova AI significantly lowers your operational and recovery costs.
🤖
AI-Powered Scoring
ML models predict repayment likelihood and prioritize accounts intelligently — getting smarter with every interaction your team has.
🔒
Compliance-First
Built-in guardrails ensure every automated interaction stays within regulatory boundaries — protecting your organization at every step.
