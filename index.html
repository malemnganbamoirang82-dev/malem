Flagship Product

Meet Recova AI

The AI platform that reduces late payments for MFIs and NBFCs — by automatically following up on every borrower, detecting every promise, and making sure every rupee owed comes back.

Powered by
Malem

Every promise tracked.
Every rupee followed up.

When your borrower says "main kal dunga," Recova AI hears it, logs it, and follows through — automatically. No agent needed. No broken promise slipping through.

🎯
Auto PTP Detection
When the AI calls or messages a borrower and they commit to a date, Recova AI detects the Promise to Pay instantly and logs it — no manual entry, no missed commitments.
🔔
Timely Follow-Ups & Reminders
Automated WhatsApp messages and AI calls go out at the right time — day before, day of, day after. Borrowers are reminded before they forget, not chased after they've gone cold.
📊
Live PTP Tracker Dashboard
A real-time view of every promise — Honored, Broken, Pending. Your collections head sees exactly which accounts need attention before they age past the 30 DPD mark.
💳
Instant Payment Links
Payment links sent in the same message as the follow-up — so when a borrower is ready to pay, they can do it in one tap, instantly.
📞
WhatsApp-First, AI Call Escalation
Every follow-up starts with a low-cost WhatsApp nudge. Only unresponsive borrowers get an AI voice call — keeping your infra costs minimal without missing anyone.
🔒
RBI & TRAI Compliant
DND checks, calling hour restrictions, and consent management built in — every interaction stays within RBI collections guidelines. Fully compliant, fully automated.

How It Works

From Due Date to
Payment Received.

Recova AI runs the entire follow-up loop — so your team only sees outcomes, not work.

01
📋

Connect Your Portfolio

Upload your borrower list or connect your LMS. Recova AI maps every account, due date, and outstanding amount instantly.

02
💬

AI Contacts Borrowers

WhatsApp reminders go first — low cost, high reach. Non-responders get an AI voice call that speaks in their dialect, naturally.

03
🎯

PTP Auto-Detected & Tracked

When a borrower commits — "main 15 tarikh ko dunga" — Recova AI logs it instantly. The PTP tracker updates in real time. No agent entry needed.

04
💸

Follow Up Until Paid

On promise date, a reminder goes out with a payment link. Borrower pays in one tap. You're notified. NPA clock stops. Done.
